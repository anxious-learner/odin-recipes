How To Make Waffles

The best waffles you've ever had and you will make them yourself! This recipe is so not difficult that even a pigeon can make it! Although, this pigeon does need to know how to use a waffle maker, so your help is very welcome.

With preparation time only 10 minutes, cooking time just 20 minutes you can make yourself 19-20 waffles! How insane is that!?

List of Ingredients

  • 250g butter
  • 200g sugar
  • 2 packets vanilla sugar (16g) or other vanilla flavoring
  • 5 eggs
  • 500g flour
  • 2 tablespoons baking powder
  • 350-400ml milk

Steps

After gathering all those awesome things, you can begin the actual cooking you've been wanting to do since you opened this silly little web page. Now, listen, here's what you need to do:

  1. Beat the eggs till creamy.
  2. Gradually add all the sugar and the vanilla flavoring.
  3. Melt and then mix in the butter.
  4. Add the baking powder.
  5. Add the milk and flour in alteration. Stir constantly while doing so.
  6. Blend and mix until you got a smooth dough. Use the highest speed of your mixer.
  7. Let the dough rest for a couple of minutes. Turn on and preheat your waffle maker, then pour in the dough. Bake inside the waffle maker till golden brown.

Enjoy!
